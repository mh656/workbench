Design Specifics: Study in Form

设计专题研究

This course is a workshop focusing on the conception of a simple publication showcasing studies in composition. This course is mainly taught in English.

Goals
  • Build/enhance student’s sensibility to composition;
  • Learn to work at a large scale;
  • Enhance signification within a sequence of images;
  • Develop attitude;
  • Play + experiment within strict constraints.

    • Requirements
  • Student who has a minimum training in design and art.

    • Credits
    3 credits

    Required Materials
  • Laptop: preferably a Macbook
  • Adobe InDesign software
  • Adobe Illustrator software
  • Easy access to a laser printer
  • Electronic mouse
  • Digital storage device
  • VPN account
  • A1 sheets of paper
  • Non-permanent glue stick
  • Rubber cement
  • Non-permanent tape
  • Double-sided tape
  • Adhesive Eraser
  • Spraymount
  • Sketchbook
  • Metal ruler
  • Bone folder
  • Large 45 deg and 60 deg set squares
  • X-acto knife and blades
  • Cutting mat
  • Variety of papers suitable for the project
  • Stapler with staples

    • Attendance
    Attend to all classes is essential. Un-excused absences will result in faults to the participation evaluation. Keep me updated if you need to miss class. In any case, the students are responsible for being up-to-date. Being late will be perceived the same way.

    Since our class periods are very long (2:30–7:45PM), we will have sections in which it will be possible for you to leave the classroom to do research elsewhere, whether it is for production or conceptualization.

    Evaluation
    You will be evaluated according to your overall engagement in the projects. Your active participation during the semester will also be taken in account.

  • Project (70%)
  • Class participation (+ anticipation)(30%).

    • Assignment Criteria (General)
  • Autonomy, authorship
  • Originality / point of view / attitude
  • Formal approach
  • Execution shows technical skills
  • Give your idea a chance
  • Strangeness factor (Extra points) (?)
